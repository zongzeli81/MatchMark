How it works
Three steps to your match
Step 01 📋

Post your have or need

Tell us what you're offering or looking for. A skill, a space, a service, a good — anything goes.

Step 02 🤖

AI finds your match

Our matching engine scans every listing and surfaces the most relevant people nearby based on shared tags and context. You'll be notified the moment a match is found — and your listing stays in our database permanently, so a match can find you anytime.

Step 03 🤝

Connect and exchange

We introduce you directly. From there, you handle the details on your own terms — no middleman, no platform fees.
